## The secrecy myth

Most quant secrecy protects capacity-constrained execution details, not ideas. The ideas themselves — factor construction, regime detection, validation methodology — improve dramatically when exposed to peer review.

## What we share

We publish factor research, evaluation methodology, and tooling. We keep execution infrastructure private. That line has served open-source communities well for decades.
